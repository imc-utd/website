Our Values


MISSION: Investing is a crucial tool that not only serves as a great educational experience but as a major method of financial mobility, an opportunity often overlooked for students in college.


VISION: IMC aims to provide UT Dallas with a community for students interested in learning about investing to come together, increasing our local community's financial literacy.

Intro Course on Trading

Recurs every semester as biweekly workshops

Welcome to all members who wish to enter the stock market without prior experience. We take students step by step from just opening a brokerage to intermediate-level trading. Students are encouraged to progress through the entire semester of workshops.

Published Analyst Reports

Posted biweekly on Discord

Our experienced market analysts publish reports on their own expertise areas regularly for members to read and make trades based off of. Our analyst reports do not serve as financial advice but as reference material posted by our organization.

Student Mentorship Program

Recurs every semester through an application

Students learn directly under the mentorship of our experienced market analysts gain valuable knowledge and tips about the market while under direct supervision. Opportunities to join as an experienced analyst after completing the program are available.
